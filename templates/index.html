Cancer Research Project

Extract and analyze Cancer data by showing which states have the most cancer cases and the types of cancer present, along with age, testing, and race.


We start the project focusing in the United States Cancer Data. After displaying several visuals that show different cancer statistics, we specifacilly focus on Wisconsin data.


Once we analize Winsconsin data, we are able to create a machine learning algorithm to determine the diagnostic of a cancer patient once we previoulsly established his/her main symtoms.
